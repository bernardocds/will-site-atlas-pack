Frequent Inquiries

What is your Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)? expand_more

For standard items, MOQs start at 10,000 units. Custom printed orders typically require 25,000-50,000 units depending on the product complexity and material.

Do you provide design support? expand_more

Yes, our in-house engineering team provides dielines and layout templates. We can also assist in optimizing your artwork for high-speed industrial printing.

How are shipping costs handled? expand_more

We offer both DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) and FOB terms. Most of our clients prefer DDP where we handle all customs, duties, and final delivery to their door.

What are your payment terms? expand_more

Standard B2B terms are 50% deposit upon order and 50% upon bill of lading. Net-30 terms are available for established corporate accounts upon credit approval.
